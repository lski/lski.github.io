Privacy and Cookies

Cookies are used to improve the quality of the website.

To this end we use cookies to analyse the performance of the website and any errors that occur, so that these issues can be fixed or improved. To enable us to do this we use analytic services provided by Microsoft called appInsights, which observes how you use the website in an anonymous way. We do not sell this information to third parties.

Currently we also do not run adverts on our website, therefore no cookies are used for that purpose. If that was to change we will update this privacy policy and request you agree to cookie usage again.
